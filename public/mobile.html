Terms of Service

Effective March 2026 · New York, NY · hello@davincii.co

Overview

These Terms govern your use of the Davincii platform, operated by Davincii LLC, organized under the laws of New York. Davincii provides music publishing administration — registering your compositions and collecting royalty income on your behalf. Artists retain 100% copyright ownership at all times.

Publishing Administration Agreement

No upfront fees. We take 15% only of what we actually collect for you.

Use of royalty collection services requires a separate Administration Agreement covering the 15% fee, quarterly accounting, exclusive admin rights during the term, and a 12-month collection tail period after termination.

Artist Responsibilities

You must provide accurate song metadata including titles, co-writer splits, ISRC/ISWC codes, and PRO affiliation. Davincii is not liable for missed royalties caused by inaccurate information.

Payments

Quarterly statements within 45 days of quarter close. Net Receipts paid when balance exceeds $50 via ACH, PayPal, or Stripe. Payment credentials are handled by Stripe — we never store bank or card numbers.

Termination

Either party may terminate upon 30 days' written notice. Following termination, Davincii continues collecting for 12 months, then remits your final balance.

Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of New York. Disputes go first to mediation, then binding arbitration in New York under AAA Commercial Rules.
