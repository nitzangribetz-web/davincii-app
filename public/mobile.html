Common questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I keep my copyrights?+
Yes, always. Davincii administers your rights — we never acquire or own them. You retain 100% copyright ownership at all times.
How do I get paid?+
We render quarterly statements and send payouts via ACH, PayPal, or Stripe directly from your artist portal once your balance exceeds $50.
I'm already with ASCAP or BMI — do I need this?+
Yes. Davincii works alongside your PRO. We cover the publishing side — mechanical, digital performance, and neighboring rights that your PRO doesn't handle.
What royalties do you collect?+
Performing rights (ASCAP, BMI, SESAC), mechanical royalties (MLC, Songtrust), digital performance (SoundExchange), and neighboring rights across 12+ international territories.
Can I leave at any time?+
Yes, 30 days written notice. We'll collect royalties generated during your term for 12 months after, then remit your final balance.
Is there a minimum catalog size?+
No. We work with artists at any stage — whether you have 1 song or 100. Every release deserves to be fully registered and earning.
