The process

should be effortless.

We handle the entire publishing administration pipeline — from registration to collection — so you can focus on making music.

01

Create your free account and submit your songs — titles, ISRC codes, co-writer splits, and release dates. We handle everything from there. No upfront cost, no contracts that lock you in.

The problem

Billions in royalties go uncollected every year.

Most independent artists never see the full value of their music because royalty collection is fragmented across dozens of platforms, each with their own registration requirements and payout cycles. A song can generate income from performing rights, digital performance rights, and mechanical rights — and miss out on all three simply because no one was watching.

  • Non-exclusive grant of administrative rights for the duration of the term

Payments & Accounting

Quarterly Accounting Statements are rendered within 45 days following the close of each calendar quarter. Net Receipts are remitted concurrently, provided the balance exceeds $50.00. Payments are processed via ACH, PayPal, Stripe, or other agreed electronic methods. Either party may terminate upon 30 days' written notice. Following termination, Davincii will render a final Accounting Statement and remit all Net Receipts within 60 days. Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026  ·  Questions: info@davincii.co
Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Davincii LLC collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use the Davincii platform and artist portal. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

When you register or use the Service, we collect:

  • Full name and artist/professional name
  • Email address and PRO affiliation (ASCAP, BMI, SESAC, or other)
  • Composition metadata: song titles, co-writer names, ownership splits, ISRC and ISWC codes, release dates
  • Payment information — processed through Stripe. We do not store card or bank account numbers directly.
  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and session data
  • Correspondence and communications with Davincii

We may also receive information from third-party performing rights organizations, collection societies, and distribution partners in connection with administering your royalties. This information is used solely to fulfill our administration obligations.

How We Use Your Information
  • Registering your compositions with PROs, collection societies, and other applicable platforms
  • Collecting, processing, and disbursing royalty income on your behalf
  • Providing access to your artist portal and quarterly accounting statements
  • Processing payouts via Stripe (ACH, PayPal, or other agreed methods)
  • Communicating about your account, royalty activity, and platform updates
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Improving and securing the platform
How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing and artist payouts via Stripe Connect. Stripe's privacy policy governs their handling of payment data.
  • PROs & collection societies — performing rights organizations and collection platforms as needed for composition registration and royalty collection
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — for hosting and platform operations
  • Legal authorities — when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Davincii or its users

Davincii's products are ad-free. We do not share data with advertisers or use third-party advertising cookies.

Stripe & Payment Data

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. Davincii does not collect, store, or process credit card numbers or bank account numbers. When you connect a payment method via Stripe Connect, that data is transmitted directly to and stored by Stripe. Davincii only receives confirmation of successful transactions and basic payout details.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to fulfill obligations under the Administration Agreement, including the 12-month Collection Tail Period following termination. Royalty accounting records are retained for a minimum of 3 years. You may request deletion of your account by contacting info@davincii.co; however, we may retain certain records as required by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or request portability of your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@davincii.co. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under GDPR. Our legal basis for processing your data is performance of a contract, legitimate interests, and legal compliance. You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption in transit and access controls. No system is entirely secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact info@davincii.co and we will promptly delete it.
