Common questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I keep my copyrights?+
Yes, always. Davincii administers your rights — we never acquire or own them. You retain 100% copyright ownership of all your compositions at all times.
How do I get paid?+
We render quarterly accounting statements and send payouts via ACH transfer, PayPal, or Stripe. You can request payment directly from your artist portal once your balance exceeds $50.
What if I'm already registered with ASCAP or BMI?+
Great — you should be. Davincii works alongside your existing PRO membership. We handle the publishing side of your registrations and collect mechanical, digital performance, and neighboring rights royalties that your PRO may not cover.
What types of royalties do you collect?+
We collect performing rights royalties (via ASCAP, BMI, SESAC), mechanical royalties (via MLC and Songtrust), digital performance royalties (via SoundExchange), and neighboring rights through international collection societies in 12+ territories.

How long does registration take?+
Most registrations are completed within 2–4 weeks. Some international collection societies can take longer. We'll keep your portal updated as each registration is confirmed.
What information do I need to submit?+
Song titles, release dates, co-writer names and ownership splits, ISRC codes, and your PRO affiliation. The more complete your metadata, the faster and more accurately we can register and collect.
Is there a minimum catalog size?+
No. We work with artists at any stage — whether you have 1 song or 100. Every release deserves to be fully registered and earning.
