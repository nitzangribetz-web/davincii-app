The process

Collecting royalties
should be effortless.

We handle the entire publishing administration pipeline — from registration to collection — so you can focus on making music.

01
You sign up & share your catalog

Create your free account and submit your songs — titles, ISRC codes, co-writer splits, and release dates. We handle everything from there.

02
We register your music everywhere

Davincii registers your songs with ASCAP, BMI, SoundExchange, the MLC, and Songtrust. Most artists miss royalties simply because their music was never registered properly — we fix that.

03
We track & collect every quarter

Performing rights royalties, digital performance royalties, mechanical royalties — we audit every platform every quarter and collect on your behalf.

04
You get paid directly

Log in to your Davincii portal, see exactly what was collected and from where, and claim your balance via ACH, PayPal, or Stripe. We take 15% of what we collect — that's it.
