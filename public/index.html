Everything you need to know.

Do I keep my copyrights?+
Yes, always. Davincii administers your rights — we never acquire or own them. You retain 100% copyright ownership of all your compositions at all times.
How do I get paid?+
We render quarterly accounting statements and send payouts via ACH, PayPal, or Stripe. You can request payment from your artist portal once your balance exceeds $50.
What if I'm already registered with ASCAP or BMI?+
Great — you should be. Davincii works alongside your existing PRO membership. We handle the publishing side and collect mechanical, digital performance, and neighboring rights your PRO may not cover.
What types of royalties do you collect?+
Performing rights (ASCAP, BMI, SESAC), mechanical royalties (MLC), digital performance royalties (SoundExchange), and neighboring rights through international collection societies worldwide.
Can I leave at any time?+
Yes. Either party can terminate with 30 days written notice. Following termination, we continue collecting royalties generated during your term for 12 months, then remit your final balance.
Is there a minimum catalog size?+
No. We work with artists at any stage — whether you have 1 song or 100. Every release deserves to be fully registered and earning.

About Your Statements

Quarterly statements are formal accounting documents showing all royalties collected on your behalf, Davincii's 15% commission, and the net amount paid to you. They are generated within 15 days of each quarter's close.

These documents are suitable for use with your accountant, manager, or legal counsel, and as proof of income for visa applications, grant programs, or financial institutions.

Privacy Policy
Effective: January 1, 2025  ·  Davincii LLC
Introduction

Davincii LLC ("Davincii", "we", "us") operates the music publishing administration platform available at davincii.co and through our artist portal. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect
  • Identity and contact information (name, email address, date of birth, postal address)
  • Artist information (stage name, PRO affiliation, IPI/CAE number)
  • Song and catalog data (titles, co-writer splits, ISRC codes, ISWC numbers, release dates)
  • Payment and tax information (processed through Stripe; W-9 / W-8BEN data)
  • Account usage data (login activity, portal usage, dashboard interactions)
  • Correspondence and communications with Davincii
How We Use Your Information
  • Registering your compositions with PROs, SoundExchange, MLC, and other collection societies
  • Collecting, processing, and disbursing royalty income on your behalf
  • Providing access to your artist portal and quarterly accounting statements
  • Processing payouts via Stripe (ACH, PayPal, or other agreed methods)
  • Communicating about your account, royalty activity, and platform updates
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service
How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing and artist payouts via Stripe Connect
  • PROs & collection societies — ASCAP, BMI, SESAC, SoundExchange, MLC, and other organizations for composition registration and royalty collection
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — for hosting and platform operations
  • Legal authorities — when required by law or court order

Davincii's products are ad-free. We do not share data with advertisers or use third-party advertising cookies.

Stripe & Payment Data

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. Davincii does not collect, store, or process credit card numbers or bank account numbers. When you connect a payment method via Stripe Connect, that data is transmitted directly to and stored by Stripe.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to fulfill obligations under the Administration Agreement, including the 12-month Collection Tail Period following termination. Royalty accounting records are retained for a minimum of 3 years.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or request portability of your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@davincii.co. We will respond within 30 days.

Agreement Overview

These Terms of Service ("Terms") govern your use of the Davincii platform and music publishing administration services provided by Davincii LLC ("Davincii"). By creating an account, you agree to be bound by these Terms and our Publishing Administration Agreement.

Services

Davincii provides music publishing administration services including composition registration with performing rights organizations (PROs), mechanical licensing societies, and digital performance rights organizations; royalty collection and quarterly distribution; and an artist portal for catalog and earnings management.

Commission & Fees

Davincii charges a commission of 15% on royalties actually collected on your behalf. No upfront fees, no monthly subscription fees, and no registration costs. You are only charged when Davincii successfully collects royalties for you.

If Davincii collects nothing, you owe nothing.

Copyright Ownership

You retain 100% copyright ownership of all your compositions at all times. Davincii is granted a limited, non-exclusive license to register and administer your compositions solely for the purpose of collecting royalties on your behalf. Davincii does not acquire any ownership interest in your music.

Term & Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Following termination, Davincii will continue collecting royalties generated during your term for 12 months (the "Collection Tail"), then remit your final balance less any applicable commission.

Payouts

Davincii renders quarterly accounting statements and distributes collected royalties via ACH, PayPal, or Stripe. Payouts are processed when your available balance exceeds $50. You are responsible for completing all required tax documentation (W-9 or W-8BEN) before your first payout can be processed.

Representations

By using Davincii, you represent that you have the right and authority to enter into this agreement, that the compositions you register are original works you created or co-created, and that registering them will not infringe any third-party rights.
