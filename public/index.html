Do I keep my copyrights?+

Song titles, release dates, co-writer names and ownership splits, ISRC codes, and your PRO affiliation. The more complete your metadata, the faster and more accurately we can register and collect.

No. We work with artists at any stage — whether you have 1 song or 100. Every release deserves to be fully registered and earning.

Publishing Administration Agreement

Use of Davincii's royalty collection services requires execution of a separate Music Publishing Administration Agreement. Key terms include:

  • Non-exclusive grant of administrative rights for the duration of the term

Payments & Accounting

Quarterly Accounting Statements are rendered within 45 days following the close of each calendar quarter. Net Receipts are remitted concurrently, provided the balance exceeds $50.00. Payments are processed via ACH, PayPal, Stripe, or other agreed electronic methods. Either party may terminate upon 30 days' written notice. Following termination, Davincii will render a final Accounting Statement and remit all Net Receipts within 60 days.   • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service
